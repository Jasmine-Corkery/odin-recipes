Bacon & Halloumi Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti carbonara with a twist! Swap the bacon out with the savoury, creamy halloumi and you've got yourself a silky halloumi carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • Halloumi
  • Bacon
  • Light single cream
  • Italian Spices

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti
  2. Cook the halloumi and bacon and add the spices and cream to the pan.
  3. Mix the spaghetti with the halloumi/bacon
  4. Serve
